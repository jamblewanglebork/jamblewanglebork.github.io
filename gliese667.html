Name : Gliesse-667Cb
        
        Exoplanet type : Neptune like
        
        Discovery year : 2009 
        
        Key facts:

        Gliese 667 Cb is an exoplanet orbiting 
        the star Gliese 667 C, which is part of a 
        triple star system in the constellation Scorpius. 
        
        Gliese 667 C is an M-dwarf, cooler and 
        less luminous than our Sun, 
        which means its habitable zone is much closer to the star.

        The planet has a relatively short orbital 
        period of about 7 days.
        
        Gliese 667 is about 23.6 light-years away from Earth, 
        making it relatively close in cosmic terms.
